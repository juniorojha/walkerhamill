Mark Richards

Mark is a Partner at Walker Hamill, having joined in 1999. He is also part of the management team which successfully completed the MBO of Walker Hamill in 2002 and the secondary buyout in 2005. Mark spent his early career within the insolvency division of a leading accountancy firm, before embarking on a career in recruitment. He is focused on accountancy & finance, corporate strategy and corporate development jobs across a wide range of sectors including media, technology, telecommunications, FMCG and property.
