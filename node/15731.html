Ref: TS 46135

Infrastructure Asset/Portfolio Management, Director

Permanent
London
Competitive salary and bonus

This leading infrastructure investment firm makes equity and equity linked investments across North America and Europe, primarily in non-public infrastructure (transportation, energy and utilities) companies, on behalf of investment funds advised by the firm.

Description

• Assume a leading role in the management of investments (alongside existing senior team members) across transport and/or utility/energy sectors, which will include:
- representing funds on portfolio company boards and day-to day interaction with portfolio company management;
- driving investment performance at asset and portfolio level to meet and exceed investment cases;
- responsibility for portfolio company and investment reporting;
• As a secondary responsibility, the role will involve origination of potential investment opportunities, utilising complementary relationship networks;
• Support for the evaluation of investment opportunities originated by other team members.

Skills

• Excellent academic record, educated to degree level
• Experience in the asset management of diverse portfolios
• Experience in the execution of M&A transactions and post transaction integration / handover would be helpful
• Track record of P&L responsibility for businesses
• Technical expertise in a transportation and/ or utility/energy sector would be preferred
• Open personality with key attributes of integrity, results driven approach, able to develop effective working relationships, excellent communication and good problem-solving skills
• Diligent and thoughtful approach to risk management, be a team player with sound judgement
